Tips for Best Results

Recommended Practices

  • Provide specific technical constraints early in the journey to ensure AI alignment with your existing infrastructure.

  • Mention affected applications and business units so the AI can map dependencies from your portfolio automatically.

  • Answer every clarifying question thoroughly — each response sharpens the architecture and prevents rework in later phases.

Common Pitfalls

  • Don't use generic titles like "New Project." Use clear intent-based names for better AI context retention across steps.

  • Avoid skipping the "Map Landscape" phase — accurate current-state maps are critical for trade-off analysis and risk assessment.

  • Do not bypass ARB Submission for production deployments. The governance workflow maintains audit compliance and traceability.
