Tips for Best Results

Recommended Practices

  • Provide specific technical constraints early to ensure AI alignment with your infrastructure.
  • Mention affected applications and business units so the AI can map dependencies automatically.
  • Answer every clarifying question thoroughly — each response sharpens the architecture.

Common Pitfalls

  • Don't use generic titles like "New Project." Use intent-based names for better AI context.
  • Avoid skipping "Map Landscape" — accurate current-state maps are critical for trade-offs.
  • Do not bypass ARB Submission for production deployments — governance maintains audit compliance.
